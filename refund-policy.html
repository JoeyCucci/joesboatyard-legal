Refund Policy for Joe's Boat Yard

Effective Date: August 10, 2025
Last Updated: August 10, 2025

1. Overview

Joe's Boat Yard, operated by FamilyTableApp LLC, serves as a marketplace platform connecting buyers and sellers of boat parts and marine equipment. This policy explains our refund procedures for platform fees and marketplace transactions.

Contact: joesboatyardapp@gmail.com

2. Platform Fees - Non-Refundable

2.1 Listing Fees

The following platform fees are NON-REFUNDABLE:
• Premium Listings: $5/month - No refunds once activated
• Featured Listings: $25-$60 (1-4 weeks) - No refunds once published
• Listing Bumps: $5 per bump - No refunds once processed
• Business Account: $99/month base + $15/extra listing - No refunds

2.2 No Refunds for User Error

We do NOT provide refunds for:
• Accidental listing deletion by user
• User changes of mind after payment
• Duplicate listings created by user
• Incorrect listing information entered by user
• Poor listing performance or lack of sales

2.3 Technical Issues - Refund Eligible

We WILL provide refunds only for:

  • Confirmed technical failures on our platform preventing listing publication
  • Payment processing errors on our end
  • Platform downtime exceeding 24 consecutive hours

3. Marketplace Transaction Disputes

3.1 Our Role

⚠️ Joe's Boat Yard is a platform only. We do not:
• Handle refunds between buyers and sellers
• Mediate transaction disputes
• Guarantee item quality, shipping, or delivery
• Process returns or exchanges

3.2 Buyer-Seller Responsibility

All transaction disputes must be resolved directly between buyers and sellers, including:

  • Item not as described
  • Shipping delays or damage
  • Non-delivery of items
  • Quality issues
  • Returns and exchanges

3.3 Dispute Resolution Process

  1. Direct Communication: Buyers and sellers should communicate directly to resolve issues
  2. Independent Resolution: Parties may pursue legal remedies independently
  3. Platform Removal: We may remove listings or users for fraudulent activity

4. Payment Processing

4.1 Stripe Transactions

  • All marketplace payments are processed by Stripe, Inc.
  • Stripe handles buyer-seller payment disputes according to their policies
  • Chargeback disputes are managed by Stripe
  • We do not have access to credit card information

4.2 Platform Fee Processing

  • Platform fees are charged immediately upon service activation
  • Payments are processed through Stripe
  • Payment failures will result in service suspension

5. Business Account Subscriptions

5.1 Monthly Billing

  • Business accounts are billed monthly at $99/month
  • Additional listings charged at $15/month each
  • No prorated refunds for partial months

5.2 Cancellation

  • Cancel anytime by contacting joesboatyardapp@gmail.com
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of current billing period
  • No refunds for remaining subscription time
  • Listings expire when subscription ends

6. Refund Request Process

6.1 Eligible Refund Requests

For technical issues only:

  1. Email: joesboatyardapp@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Refund Request - Technical Issue"
  3. Include: Order details, issue description, supporting evidence
  4. Response within 3-5 business days

6.2 Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refunds issued to original payment method via Stripe
  • Refund confirmation sent by email

7. Chargeback Policy

7.1 Unauthorized Chargebacks

Filing unauthorized chargebacks for legitimate services may result in:

  • Account suspension
  • Legal action to recover costs
  • Chargeback fees passed to user

7.2 Legitimate Disputes

For legitimate billing disputes:

  1. Contact us first at joesboatyardapp@gmail.com
  2. Allow 5 business days for response
  3. Pursue chargeback only if unresolved
